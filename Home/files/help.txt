Sima Online Story (help):

    t(text) -- Prints text
    b(name, onclick) -- Adds Button
    w(ms) -- Wait for ms
    db(key) -- Get from Sima DB
    db(key, value) -- Put into Sima DB some key=value
    scene(name, function) -- Creates Scene with given function
    goto(scenename) -- go to scene
    goto() -- refresh scene
